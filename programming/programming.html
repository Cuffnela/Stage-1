A Nanodegree and the Future

In the middle of May 2016 I started the programming nanodegree through Udacity. I am currently working to refresh and strengthen my programming skills and make the move out of teaching. This website is the product of Stage 1 of the programming nanodegree.

Background

I have been coding off and on since college. While completing my bachelor's in mathematics I completed a intro to Java course at NCSU. In grad school I had the opportunity to take an advanced programming course. This course moved through four different languages in one semester. We wrote programs in Java, C, C++ and python during the course. Most of the programming I have done has been in MATLAB. MATLAB is a numerical computing enviorment and programming language. I used MATLAB for classwork and to run simulations for my master's thesis.
